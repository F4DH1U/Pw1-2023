Our History

The history of coffee dates back to centuries of old oral tradition in modern-day Ethiopia and Yemen

It was already known in Mecca in the 15th century.Also, in the 15th century, Sufi monasteries in Yemen employed coffee as an aid to concentration during prayers.

Coffee later spread to the Levant in the early 16th century;it caused some controversy on whether it was halal in Ottoman and Mamluk society.
